Gene Calc

This application is in beta-version, so if you find a bug or error please let us know contact@gene-calc.pl

The Gene-Calc is a constantly developed tool dedicated for the needs of calculations related to biological sciences, particularly in field of genetics. The open access tool offers:
  • study of the genetic equilibrium
  • extended tests from the chi-square family for contingency tables of any size
  • determination of PIC and H values for both codominant and dominant markers
